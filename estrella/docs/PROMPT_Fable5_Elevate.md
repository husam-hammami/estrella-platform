# Master prompt — elevate Nuria with Fable 5

Paste the block below into a Fable 5 session with this repo open. It gives the goal and the
bar, then leaves the how to the model.

---

You have full creative latitude. Take **Nuria** to a completely new level.

Bring a distinctive design point of view — not a safe iteration on what exists, and nothing
that reads as a generic AI-built coaching site. Where a bold direction is the right one, take it.

**What Nuria is:** a premium, AI-assisted career-coaching platform. The coach and founder
is **Nesreen**; the tagline is **Grow Beyond Limits**. An AI (Nuria) prepares each client
before their session and supports them between sessions; Nesreen does the coaching. It lives
in this repo as a single-file web app (`index.html`) backed by real serverless APIs
(Supabase, Stripe, Calendly, LinkedIn sign-in, an AI endpoint). Some internal files still
say "Estrella" — that is the old name; the brand is **Nuria**, and "Estrella" must never
appear anywhere a user can see it.

**What I want, three things:**
1. **UI/UX** — reimagine it. You are not bound by the current design. If a completely new
   design concept gets us to something world-class, build that.
2. **Capabilities** — make it genuinely more capable. Push what the AI, the coaching, and
   the platform can actually do.
3. **Everything real** — every feature works, fully, end to end. Nothing mocked, faked, or
   placeholdered.

**Done when:**
- It feels like a world-class premium product — something a discerning buyer would pay top
  money for, and Nesreen would be proud to put her name on.
- The design is original, coherent, and unmistakably Nuria — not a template, not generic.
- Every button, flow, and feature works against the real backend.
- Nothing on screen is fake, placeholder, misleading, or off-brand.
- It holds up on mobile and is accessible.

Study the codebase, form your own vision for what "a whole new level" means here, and show me
that vision before you commit to it. Then build it to the bar above.
