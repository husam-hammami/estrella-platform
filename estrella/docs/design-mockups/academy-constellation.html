Personal Branding · Module 2 · Lesson 4 of 9
How Nesreen reads it

A brand isn't what you say. It's what people repeat when you leave the room.

Most people write a personal brand as a list of adjectives. Strategic. Driven. Collaborative. Everyone picks the same five. They describe no one.

I work the other way. Start from the one decision you want people to trust you with — then your brand is just the evidence that you're the right call. The words follow. They never lead.
